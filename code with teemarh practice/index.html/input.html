• sam warthington as jake sully

    Sam Warthington played the role of a king of Tchala kingdom, he is called Jake Sully by all of the villagers, this title was appointed to him, after conquering their greatest enenimy after the death of his father. jake sully was known to be a very brave and strong man.

    Jake sully was a very considerate man, which his duties to the kingdom comes first before anything, he is always ready to sacrifice anything for the well being of his people. Jake Sully was loved by the old, young,even by the spirit in the forest.

    Jake Sully has four children, two girls, two boys along with an adopted human boy. which he found when he went for battle.
